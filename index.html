Red Paper Clip Project

1st Update 9/21/2021

I have to do the "Red Paperclip" project for school and make blog posts about it so here is the rundown:


  1. 1) Start with a red paperclip
  2. 2) Barter your way to (hopefully) better things
  3. 3) End up with something (again hopefully) better than a red paperclip




Ideally I might be able to get something of a little more value but the man that popularized this trend got a house so that is what I'm shooting for. Realistically though I will probably only end up with like a phone charger or something of similar value.

I will probably mostly stay at school and in my neighborhood but I might explore the slimy depths of facebook marketplace if I feel bold enough to. I will try to only trade with people who have something of higher value than I, but that most likely will fall through at some point or another.

Anyways on to the actual trade I did:

The first trade I did this week was I traded the paperclip to my dad for a piece of paper. While this might not seem like a great trade I can use the piece of paper and fold it which is an easy way to create value. I will try my best to keep doing this as it could help boost along my trading adventure.

Anywho I then folded the piece of paper into a little dragon which then allowed me to trade it for an old amazon box that my mother didn't need anymore. Again, I used my skills to make it into a little cardboard airplane which is where I am at now.



I feel like I am in a decent position, and I have already made a lot of progress from the paper clip i started out with.
